About Neuro Next Door

Neuro Next Door is a welcoming space where complex neuroscience concepts are broken down into simple, engaging insights for everyone. Whether you’re curious about how the brain works or eager to explore the latest discoveries, you'll find information here that’s easy to understand and relevant to daily life.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between scientific research and everyday understanding. We believe that everyone deserves access to knowledge about how the mind works, and Neuro Next Door exists to make neuroscience ideas approachable and meaningful.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Simplified Explanations of neuroscience concepts and research.
  • Practical Insights on topics like memory, emotions, and perception.
  • Breaking Down the Basics: A beginner-friendly series on foundational neuroscience concepts.
  • Featured Articles: In-depth explorations of specific topics, explained for a general audience.

Meet the Author

I’m Zeba Quadri, a Cognitive Neuroscience student with a passion for making science accessible. With a background in research and a love for teaching, I started Neuro Next Door to share the wonders of brain science in a way that’s clear and enjoyable. I hope this blog becomes a place where curiosity and understanding meet, inspiring readers to learn more about the mind.
